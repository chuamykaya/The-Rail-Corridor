Nature

The Rail Corridor leads through a stretch of greenery along the upper Bukit Timah area.

If you're up for it, you can take a detour to Bukit Timah hill, or exit at Fuyong estate to access the scenic Singapore Quarry site.

The Rail Corridor also passes by the Clementi forest, one of the biggest areas of wildlife habitat in Singapore.

Flanked with scenes of greenery on the left and right, walking through the Rail Corridor is definitely a refreshing break from Singapore's concrete jungle.
